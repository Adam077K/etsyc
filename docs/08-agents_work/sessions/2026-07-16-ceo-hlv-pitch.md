## The pitch (final)
- **Name: KOL** — Hebrew for *voice* (working name; **trademark/domain check REQUIRED before any public/API use** — see Etsyc entry in DECISIONS).
- **Tagline: "Every maker, finally heard."**
- **Idea:** a new kind of marketplace built around the real human behind the product. In a world where you can't tell machine-made from human-made, the maker's own **voice + world** (their colors, craft, story) is what earns trust and connection. Setup is one short honest recording; the maker stays the creative author, tech does the heavy lifting they were never built for. Trust = provenance ("tap any line, hear the second she said it"), not an unfakeable claim.

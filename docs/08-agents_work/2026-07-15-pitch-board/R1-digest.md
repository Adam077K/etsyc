# R1 — Board Digest (6 personas, independent)

**Verdicts:** broad-adversary RECONSIDER · customer-voice RECONSIDER · risk-modeler PWC · strategist PWC · visionary PWC · architect PWC.
**Read:** No KILL. Direction survives; the *current framing* has three convergent, fatal-if-unfixed weaknesses — and the board converged on one reframe that fixes most of them.

## The three knives (unanimous targets)
1. **The trust claim is a self-own.** "AI bounded by design — can't fake the human" is unenforceable hand-wave. A judge guts it: *"You fight AI-faked stories with AI-made stores?"* It also IS the platform-declared trust Q4 pre-rejected. (adversary, risk FM-2/FM-4, architect, strategist)
2. **Seller apathy — the prior board's #1 objection was overwritten, not answered.** Whole funnel needs apathetic makers to record interviews + co-create brands. Who does this at scale? (adversary, risk FM-1, strategist)
3. **"Scroll talking-head interviews" is the wrong buyer UX.** Gen Z swipes past monologues; "that's just Instagram"; ~70% price-check on Google in 10s and leak to the maker's own Shopify. Dies week 2. (customer-voice)

## The gift — the one reframe that fixes the most
**The un-fakeable atom = the maker's own VOICE + HANDS, cited to timecode.** Replace "AI bounded" with: *"Every word in the store traces to a timecode in the maker's own recorded voice — nothing the AI writes exists without a human source you can hear."* Killer interaction (and the words-only demo): **tap any headline → hear the exact second she said it.** This answers Q4 (her voice, not a badge), defuses the hypocrisy attack (FM-4), makes it feel-able in a words-only pitch (FM-5), and is buildable/demoable by Jul-24 (architect). Customer-voice adds: the *feed* should be the **thing being made (hands/material/time) with the voice as the audio track** — un-fakeable + connective in the same 6 seconds. (Vindicates the founder's earlier making-first instinct.)

## The raise (visionary)
"A new way to shop" is an order of magnitude too small — reads as "cute." The real primitive: **verified human presence as the scarce asset in a synthetic economy — the human-provenance layer for the post-AI internet.** Loneliness isn't a side-benefit, it's the market. Etsy = Day 1, not the ceiling. Let the room *discover* this (file 07 coaching). Candidate vision line: *"The last thing AI can't fake is the person on the other side. We're building the layer of the internet where that person is real, present, and chosen — starting with the things you buy."*

- **Premium:** founder chose "connection earns a premium." Strategist: cut it (unproven, mentors bite). Customer: ~10–15%, gifts-only, zero for self-purchase after month 2, leaks to maker-direct. → founder's call.
- **Team-shaped holes:** strategist — currently a solo-founder monologue; plant lanes (capture pipeline, buyer UX, trust/citation engine) classmates can own.
- **Cut seller-tooling airtime:** strategist/risk — AI store-builder/co-creation → one line in "how we start"; buyer value is the load-bearing beam.

## Steel-men worth keeping

- "Voice keeps playing while you shop the store" = human as *ambient layer*, a real UX invention. (customer)
- "AI flips from the #1 fear to what makes trust possible" — ownable inversion. (risk)
- Raw interview audio playing under shopping = un-fakeable-at-scale, cheap to verify by ear. (architect)

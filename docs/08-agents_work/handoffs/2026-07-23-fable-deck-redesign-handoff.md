## THE STORY / FLOW the deck should follow (from the HLV mentor — this is the spine)
Full breakdown: `docs/05-marketing/HLV-mentor-feedback-structure.md`. The mentor's #1 shift: **STORY is the spine, not trust/verification.** "E-commerce for the age of storytelling." Order he wants:

5. **Data / reasons why** — the 5 interviews + the public survey (69.4% of 83 said KOL sounded better than Amazon etc.). **Showcase these strongly** (feedback #4).
6. **Market** — storytelling is disconnected from shopping; social platforms (TikTok/Instagram) tried and it's not their model. For human-made goods, KOL owns the **top-right corner** (fix the axis labels — feedback #5). Anchor on **sourced $11.9B**; fold the "target opportunity" in here (feedback #7).
7. **Business model** — three elements: **platform fee + transaction fee + the tools**.
8. **How it's made possible** — introduce **the tools**: the AI **co-creation interview** in setup that lets the maker express her story/values (she stays the author).
9. **Signals / what we're missing** — a **side venture, not an A/B test**; run in a story-rich Etsy niche; measure conversion & add-to-basket; LTV is the hypothesis.
10. **Vision — at the very end** — for handmade goods, story should be front and center in everything sold in the future.

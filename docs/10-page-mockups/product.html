Noor's exact role

I dye, wring, air, and finish every throw myself. I do not cut or hem the base cloth — see partners.

Materials

100% organic cotton base (280gsm), natural indigo, plant-fixed. No synthetic dye, no fixative salts.

Process

Hand-bound shibori resist, fold-and-clamp, 6–9 dip cycles with air-oxidation between each.

Production location

Dyed in my Lisbon studio. Vat built and maintained on site.

Partners

Base cloth woven & hemmed by a family mill in Guimarães. I name them because I didn't make the cloth.

Ask the maker

Q. Does the indigo rub off on light sofas?

Noor: A little the first week, like new denim. After the first wash it's set. I'd keep it off cream linen until then.

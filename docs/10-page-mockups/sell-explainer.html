The whole path, nothing hidden

Six steps. About an afternoon. You approve every one.

  1. 01 15–20 min

    The interview

    A warm conversation on film or by voice. Seven things about you and your craft. You do the talking; nothing to fill in.

  2. 02 a few minutes

    We draft your world

    From your own words we build the whole shop — your colours, your words, your pieces. Grab a coffee, or come back later.

  3. 03 20–30 min

    You co-edit it

    Change any line, any colour, any piece — in plain language, together. Not a settings panel. A back-and-forth.

  4. 04 ~10 min

    Your voice on top

    Record a short voiceover for the pieces you want to speak to. Your real voice — buyers hear you before they see a price.

  5. 05 your pace

    You approve, section by section

    Go through it at your own speed. Send anything back as many times as you like. Nothing is live yet.

  6. 06 instant

    Publish

    You press the button — only you. Your shop goes live with your name on it, ready for the first person to walk in.

Times are honest, not hurried. You can pause after any step and pick it back up whenever you like.

Straight answers to what you're actually wondering

You're an artist. Not a web designer, not a marketer. Good — that's the whole point.

Do I need to design anything?

No. Not one colour, not one layout.

You never open a design tool or a colour picker. From what you say, we derive a look that's yours — and you nudge it in words if you want to. The design slack is ours to carry. The taste is yours.

Will it sound like me?

Yes — it's your words and your voice.

The writing is pulled from what you actually said, never invented. And your recorded voice sits on top. If we can't hear it from you, we leave it out rather than make it up.

How long does this take?

About an afternoon, start to finish — and you can stop anytime.

Roughly 15–20 minutes talking, a short break while we draft, then co-editing and approving at whatever pace feels right. Come back tomorrow if you'd rather. Your progress waits for you.

What if I hate it?

Then it doesn't go live. Simple as that.

You approve every section yourself. Nothing publishes until you say so, and you can send any part back for another pass as many times as you need. There is no version of this where you're stuck with something you don't love.

Talk for twenty minutes. Walk out with a shop that's yours.

No card needed to start. No design skill needed, ever. Just you, your craft, and a conversation.

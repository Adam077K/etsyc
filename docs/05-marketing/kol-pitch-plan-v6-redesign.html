S18Redesign — collect the reasons

Without a story, makers compete on price. That's the leak.

On the slide
One coherent, labeled argument (replaces the black funnel + the muddy $10.5B slide). Three collected reasons a handmade maker loses margin when there's no story:
▸ 20–40% price gap vs. what a told story can command (our estimate)
▸ ~3× fewer sales for shops without short-form video (our estimate)
▸ heavy dependence on paid promotion to get seen
Anchored on the real market: Etsy GMS $11.9B (FY2025), 86.6M buyers, 5.6M sellers.
Fix
#6 collect the price reasons into one slide. Accuracy: use $11.9B (sourced), not $10.5B. Every derived figure printed as "our estimate." #4 rail: remove "forced ad fees / inefficiency" — frame as a maker/market dynamic, never an Etsy indictment.
Design
Paper ground, three stacked reason-rows with terracotta magnitude bars. Sourced footnote line at the bottom.
Says
"Here's what's at stake for makers. Without a story to justify their price, they discount 20–40%, they sell a fraction of what video shops sell, and they lean on paid promotion. In an $11.9-billion market, that's a lot of value left on the table."

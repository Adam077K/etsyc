## Figures we could NOT source — DO NOT USE on stage

- **Purchases per active buyer (unit count).** Etsy publishes GMS/buyer ($122), not order count/buyer. Any "X purchases/year" number requires an unstated AOV assumption and is inventable.
- **% one-and-done Etsy buyers, from an Etsy-primary source.** Only third-party proxies exist. If S4 needs this, pivot to the habitual-decline stat (Fig #3, HIGH).
- **Q1 2026 habitual buyer count.** Only FY2025 (5.9M) is publicly stated.
- **Faire blended take rate across GMV / contribution margin.** Private company, not disclosed.
- **Etsy company-wide AOV, current.** Not cleanly disclosed in years.
- **"Keep Commerce Human" as a Kruti Patel Goyal verbatim quote in the Q1 2026 letter.** Not present. Use her "Amplify human connection" wording instead.
- **Global online-handmade-only market size.** Handicraft market reports mix on/offline. If specificity matters, anchor bottom-up on Etsy consolidated GMS ($11.9B).

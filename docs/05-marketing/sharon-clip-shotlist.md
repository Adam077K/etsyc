# Quick video ask — Two Dots × KOL

Hi Sharon — thank you so much for doing this! We're building a demo that shows the real maker behind the work, and having *actual you* on film is what makes it special.

**The whole ask is tiny: 3 short clips (about 30 seconds of footage total) + a few product photos.** Shorter is better — we only use a few seconds of each clip.

## How to film (30 seconds of setup)
- **Phone is perfect.** Film **horizontal** (turn it sideways).
- **Face a window** or any soft light — no need for anything fancy.
- **Quiet room**, phone steady (lean it on something or have someone hold it).
- **Don't perform.** Casual and real is exactly what we want — a little imperfect is better than polished. The whole point is that people can tell you're a real person.

## The 3 clips

**1. Your hands making something** — *no talking, ~10 seconds*
Just film your hands doing the actual work, up close — the material, the making. That's it. (This is the most important one and the easiest.)

**2. A quick hello** — *~10 seconds*
Look at the camera and answer: **"Who are you, and what do you make?"**
If it's easier to read a line: *"Hi, I'm Sharon — I make ______ at Two Dots."*

**3. A thank you** — *~8 seconds*
Look at the camera like you're talking to someone who just bought from you: **"Thank you so much — I really hope you love it."**

## Plus: a few product photos
Send us clear photos of a handful of pieces you sell — **the ones you already use on your Etsy shop are perfect.** We build your product pages from these, so no need to film them.

## Optional bonus (only if easy)
**Your space** — *no talking, ~8 seconds:* a slow pan around where you make things.

That's everything. Send them however's easiest (text, email, WhatsApp, Drive) — raw and unedited is perfect. Thank you again! 🙏
